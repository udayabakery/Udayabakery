✦ Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from Udaya Bakery.

How long does delivery take?▼
We typically deliver within 30–60 minutes depending on your distance. Orders placed during peak hours (evenings) may take slightly longer. You'll receive a WhatsApp update when your order is on the way!
What payment methods do you accept?▼
We accept Cash on Delivery, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and Bank Transfer. Payment is collected at the time of delivery for COD orders.
Can I cancel or modify my order?▼
You can cancel or modify your order within 5 minutes of placing it by calling us on WhatsApp at 9845406712. Once the bakers have started preparing, we cannot make changes.
Do you take custom cake orders?▼
Yes! We love making custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, and special occasions. Please place a Custom Order with at least 24 hours notice and message us on WhatsApp with your requirements and reference photos.
Is there a minimum order amount?▼
There is no minimum order for walk-ins. For home delivery, a minimum order may apply based on your distance. Free delivery is available within 2 km of our bakery in Belman.
Are your products freshly made every day?▼
Absolutely! Everything at Udaya Bakery is baked fresh daily. We do not use preservatives. Puffs, bread and snacks are baked every morning, and cakes are made to order for maximum freshness.
